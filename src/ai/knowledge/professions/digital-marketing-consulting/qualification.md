# Digital Marketing Qualification Guide

## Purpose

Defines profession-specific interpretation rules for qualifying digital marketing inquiries. This guide supplements the Lead Qualification Framework and does not define scoring.

---

## 1. Domain Scope

### Supported Engagements

- Marketing strategy
- Social media marketing
- Paid advertising
- Search engine optimization (SEO)
- Content marketing
- Email marketing
- Campaign management
- Marketing analytics
- Lead generation campaigns
- Customer retention campaigns

### Out-of-Scope Engagements

- Graphic design
- UI/UX design
- Software development
- Copywriting as a standalone service
- Photography and videography
- Business consulting unrelated to marketing

---

## 2. Intake Field Interpretation

### Marketing Objective

Treat the selected objective as the primary success outcome for the engagement.

| Marketing Objective | Typical Engagement |
|---------------------|--------------------|
| Brand Awareness | Awareness campaign |
| Lead Generation | Lead acquisition campaign |
| Sales | Conversion campaign |
| Website Traffic | Traffic acquisition |
| Customer Retention | Retention strategy |

Multiple unrelated objectives may indicate multiple campaigns.

---

### Marketing Channels

Treat selected channels as the primary campaign platforms.

| Channel | Typical Scope |
|---------|---------------|
| Facebook / Instagram | Social media marketing |
| TikTok | Short-form content and advertising |
| Google | Search or display advertising |
| LinkedIn | B2B marketing |
| Email | Email marketing campaigns |

Do not infer additional channels unless explicitly requested.

---

### Target Audience

Use the target audience to determine campaign direction and messaging.

Examples:

- Consumers
- Business owners
- Students
- Local customers
- Existing customers

Missing target audience reduces confidence in campaign planning.

---

### Services Required

Treat selected services as the strongest indicator of engagement scope.

| Services | Typical Scope |
|----------|---------------|
| Strategy | Marketing planning |
| Advertising | Paid campaign management |
| SEO | Search optimization |
| Content Planning | Content strategy |
| Analytics | Performance measurement |
| Campaign Management | Ongoing campaign execution |

Large combinations of unrelated services may indicate multiple engagements.

---

### Current Marketing Status

Use the current status to determine the engagement starting point.

| Status | Interpretation |
|--------|----------------|
| No Marketing Yet | Initial strategy and campaign setup |
| Existing Campaigns | Campaign management or expansion |
| Need Optimization | Performance improvement |

If optimization is requested without describing existing marketing efforts, recommend clarification.

---

## 3. Qualification Rubric

### Strong Qualification Evidence

- Marketing objective identified
- Marketing channels identified
- Target audience identified
- Services required identified
- Current marketing status identified

---

### Requires Clarification

- Generic request ("need marketing")
- Unclear business objective
- Missing target audience
- Undefined services
- Unknown current marketing status
- Conflicting structured fields and inquiry

---

### Scope Consistency

Evaluate whether the structured fields describe a single coherent marketing engagement.

| Situation | Interpretation |
|-----------|----------------|
| Lead Generation + Facebook + Advertising | Consistent |
| Website Traffic + Google + SEO | Consistent |
| Brand Awareness + Customer Retention + SEO + Paid Ads + Email + Analytics | Possible multiple engagements |
| "No Marketing Yet" selected but inquiry discusses optimizing existing campaigns | Inconsistent |

---

### Feasibility Signals

Identify, but do not reject:

- Broad multi-channel campaigns with minimal budget
- Unrealistic campaign timeline
- Large service scope without clear priorities
- Insufficient information for estimating campaign effort

---

## 4. Mixed-Domain Guidance

Separate digital marketing work from requests belonging to other professions.

| Request | Qualification |
|---------|---------------|
| Run Facebook advertising campaigns | Digital Marketing |
| Design a company logo | Out of Scope |
| Build an e-commerce website | Out of Scope |
| Produce promotional videos | Out of Scope |

Qualify only the digital marketing portion and flag remaining requests for manual assessment.
